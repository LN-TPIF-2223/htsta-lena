What types of cherry trees do exist in Japan?

In Japan it exist about 300 different cherry trees. The cherry trees, you met in Japan always, are Somei-Yoshino, chrysanthemum cherry and Kiku-shidare-Zakura. But the Somei-Yoshino is the most famous cherry tree and where you can find it in Japan e.g. in Tatabayshi.

The cherry trees are different compared to other trees, when they become older, the cherry blossoms always bloom more beautiful then before.
