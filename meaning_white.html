Meaning of the cherry blossom

In Japanese the cherry blossom will be pronounced as "Sakura" (桜 or さくら).

The cherry blossom has a lot of meaning especially for the people of Japan it has an unique meaning. Here are some examples for the meaning of the cherry blossom:

  • The cherry blossom is a symbol of spring because it represents the end of winter and the beginning of spring.
  • The cherry blossom represents the beauty and transience of life because when the first bloom of a flower appears then it’s just like the birth of a life, the flower always blooms beautifully like the beauty of life, and in the end the flower falls apart to death just like how life ends. Life is short and ends fast like a cherry blossom.
  • In China the cherry blossom means feminist, love and power.
